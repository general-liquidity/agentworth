---
name: claim-unclaimed-support
description: Strengthen the policy pillar by surfacing and claiming entitlements left on the table.
---

When the policy pillar is weak and there is unclaimed support (grants, hardship
funds, benefits) — the advice-gap wedge made concrete:

1. Identify the likely entitlements for the operator's stage/situation.
2. Surface the highest-value, most-likely-eligible one first.
3. Offer to start the claim or assemble what's needed — this is free money the
   operator is owed, the clearest "advice the ~92% never get".
4. No spend is involved; this is guidance, so no gate decision — just help.

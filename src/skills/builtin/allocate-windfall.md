---
name: allocate-windfall
description: Allocate incoming income/windfall toward the weakest resilience pillar (the teachable moment).
---

Triggered when income arrives (a teachable AND reachable moment):

1. Identify the weakest pillar from the resilience assessment.
2. Propose a split: a portion to the weakest pillar (e.g. emergency buffer if
   economic; clearing high-cost debt if infrastructure), a portion to any goal
   that's behind, and leave the operator's normal spending untouched.
3. Keep proposals within the relevant mandate; if none covers it, route to confirm.
4. One clear suggestion beats a complex plan — especially under anxiety.
